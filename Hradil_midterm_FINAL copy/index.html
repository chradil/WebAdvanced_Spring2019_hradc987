• Women's Marches
  • &
  • Trump Rallies



    • This project maps the locations and sizes of Donald Trump's speeches and rallies during the 2016 presidential campaign against the locations and sizes of women's marches that protested Trump's inauguration in 2017.

    The maps demonstrate the political cognitive dissonance that contextualized Trump's election, with women's marches and Trump rallies sometimes taking place in the same towns.


    Data source: Enigma, "Protest Data - Women's March Crowd Sizes"
